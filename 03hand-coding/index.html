Creating a harmonious space doesn't have to be expensive. With small, intentional changes, any home can feel like a sanctuary. Start by identifying areas in your home that feel cluttered or chaotic and take simple steps toward organization. Use natural light whenever possible. Open curtains and place mirrors to reflect brightness throughout your rooms. If you're new to these ideas, The Spruce Feng Shui guide offers practical advice to get started. Remember that how you feel in your home affects your mood and energy every day. For a deeper understanding of traditional Feng Shui principles, Qigong Energy Healing provides in-depth resources and layout tools to help you create balance.
