Who were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include:

Thomas Jefferson
Principal author of the Declaration of Independence; later became the 3rd President of the United States.
John Adams
A key advocate for independence; later served as the 2nd President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
Renowned inventor, diplomat, and statesman; helped negotiate the alliance with France and the Treaty of Paris.
John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress; known for his bold and large signature.
Samuel Adams
Cousin of John Adams; a strong voice for liberty and organizer of resistance against British rule.

These individuals played significant roles not just in signing the document, but also in shaping the early United States government and its founding principles.
